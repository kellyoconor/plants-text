TL;DR: We collect your phone number to text you plant reminders. We don't sell your data. You can delete everything anytime.

At Plant Texts, your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • Phone Number: To send you SMS messages from your plants
  • Plant Information: Names, types, and care schedules you create
  • Messages: Your conversations with plants (for improving responses)
  • Care Logs: When you water, fertilize, or care for plants

Information We Automatically Collect:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system
  • Log Data: IP address, timestamps, error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Send you plant care reminders via SMS
  • Generate AI-powered plant personality responses
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your data only with:

Service Providers:

  • Twilio: To send SMS messages (they process your phone number)
  • OpenAI: To generate plant personality responses (anonymized)
  • Hosting Providers: To store and serve our application

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We keep your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup Systems: May retain data for up to 90 days in backups
  • Legal Requirements: We may retain data longer if required by law

5. Your Rights & Choices

Access & Control:

  • View Your Data: See all data we have about you
  • Update Information: Change plant names, preferences anytime
  • Delete Account: Permanently remove all your data
  • Opt Out of SMS: Text "STOP" to stop receiving messages

Data Portability:

You can request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

6. Security

We take security seriously and use industry-standard measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure data storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Plant Texts is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

Plant Texts is based in the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the US.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what data we collect
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to opt out of data sales (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

10. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you're in the EU, you have rights under GDPR including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification (correction)
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

11. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site usage (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work.

12. Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes via:

  • SMS notification
  • Email (if provided)
  • In-app notification

Continued use after changes means you accept the new policy.

14. Contact Us

Questions, concerns, or data requests? Reach out:

Email: privacy@planttexts.com
Website: planttexts.com
Response Time: Within 30 days

15. Data Processing Details

For transparency, here's what happens to your data:

  • Phone Number: Stored encrypted, used only for SMS delivery
  • Messages: Processed by OpenAI for responses, not stored long-term
  • Care Logs: Stored to track plant health and schedule
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized and aggregated
